# Shop Strategy — EMGamer731

## North-star
The shop is **inspirational first, transactional second.** Most visitors are kids who can't transact directly. The job of the shop is to seed desire, give parents something easy to gift, and give the squad endless things to point at.

## Pricing rails
- **Stickers / digital:** $0–$8 — entry tier, often free. Lowers friction for first contact.
- **Soft accessories** (socks, beanies, mugs, mousepads): $16–$24 — pocket-money tier.
- **Tees:** $28–$32 — gifting tier.
- **Hoodies / crewnecks / sweats:** $44–$58 — premium tier.
- **Drops** (bear-charm purse, sage cardigan, syrup zip, plush): $58–$74 — collector tier.

## Margins
Target blended margin 55%+ on physical goods; 80%+ on digital. Stickers and free wallpapers are intentionally loss-leading — they grow the squad.

## Drops cadence
- **Always-on staples:** Mint slogan hoodie, Pink waffle hoodie, Black EMG cap, Tee 3-pack, Sticker sheet, Free wallpapers.
- **Monthly drops:** One headline limited piece (Sage cardigan, Bear-charm purse, Pancake slippers, etc.). Limited to 1 month or 100 units, whichever first.
- **Seasonal drops:** Holiday (Nov/Dec), Back-to-school (Aug), Summer (Jun), Spring (Apr).

## Bundle plays
- **Squad Starter Pack:** Tee + sticker sheet + free wallpapers — $32, pitched to first-timers.
- **Cozy Bundle:** Crewnecks + beanie + mug — 15% off.
- **Stream Setup:** Desk mat + mouse pad + tumbler — 10% off.

## Collection rotation rules
- 10 named collections (see `collection-structure.json`).
- Each collection has 3–6 product anchors.
- Limited drops live inside `eat-with-emm` collection (avatar callbacks).

## Storytelling
- Every product page tells a small story (collection → vibe → use case).
- Product hero images use the same illustration system as the rest of the site (consistency).
- Lifestyle imagery is shot/illustrated against breakfast-world backdrops, not generic studio.

## Channels
- **Primary:** EMGamer731 site shop (`/shop`)
- **Secondary:** TikTok shop linkout (when feasible)
- **Tertiary:** Print-on-demand fulfillment partners for soft launches; in-house production for headline drops

## Compliance + safety
- No data collection beyond order-fulfillment basics.
- Parent-friendly checkout: clear shipping/return language, plain pricing, no surprise add-ons.
- Sizing for kids (XS) and adults (2XL).
- Free wallpapers do not require email.
