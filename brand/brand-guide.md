# EMGamer731 Brand Guide

Version 1.0 — 2026-04-25

---

## 1. Brand Positioning

**EMGamer731 is the cozy-glossy creator universe of EMM — a Roblox-fashion gamer who hosts the breakfast table of the internet.**

We exist to give kids and teens a daily morning destination that feels equal parts cute café, gaming HQ, and creator backstage. Where competitors lean chaotic, we lean warm + premium. Where competitors lean generic, we lean specifically EMM — same avatar, same slogan, same morning energy, every time.

### Positioning statement
> For Roblox-loving fans aged 6–16 who want a creator they can return to every morning, EMGamer731 is the breakfast-themed creator hub that makes every visit feel like sitting at the cool table of the cafeteria — because unlike louder gaming creators, we lead with warmth, food, and a polished aesthetic kids actually want to share.

## 2. Brand Personality

| Dial | Setting |
|---|---|
| Energy | High but contained — "buzzing morning" not "spilled cereal" |
| Warmth | High — always hospitable, never sarcastic at a kid's expense |
| Polish | Premium creator — glossy, animated, never sloppy |
| Playfulness | Constant but smart — wordplay yes, irony no |
| Edge | Mild — confidence without trash-talk |
| Inclusivity | Default-on — no in-jokes that exclude new visitors |

### If EMGamer731 were a person
She is the friend who shows up to the lunch table with extra waffles for everyone, in a perfectly coordinated outfit, midway through telling you which Roblox game you absolutely have to try after school. She is dialed-in but not too cool to say good morning.

## 3. Voice & Tone Snapshot

(Full detail in `voice-and-copy-guide.md`.)

- **Headlines:** punchy, often a question, slogan-adjacent. ("What did you guys eat for breakfast today?")
- **Body copy:** warm, second-person, short sentences, minimal jargon.
- **CTAs:** verb-led, emoji-light, never shouty. ("Watch live now" not "WATCH NOW!!!")
- **Error states:** never blaming, always cute. ("This page slept in. Let's get you back to the table →")
- **Live state:** confident + present-tense. ("EMM is live — pull up a seat.")

## 4. Tagline Usage Rules

### Primary tagline
> **"What did you guys eat for breakfast today?"**

- Always sentence case. Never ALL CAPS in body — capitals only allowed inside merch graphic compositions.
- Always with the question mark.
- Never abbreviated.
- Reserved spaces: home hero, every shop product hero, OG image, stream end-card.

### Supporting taglines (rotate)
- "The cozy corner of Roblox."
- "Pull up a chair. Stream's on."
- "Breakfast Squad assemble."
- "Eat well. Game better."
- "Every morning is a drop."

### Tagline pairing
- Primary tagline + "by EMGamer731" lockup is acceptable on social.
- Never pair the primary tagline with a competing slogan in the same composition.

## 6. Typography Recommendations
See `typography-guide.md`. Summary:

- **Display:** "Fraunces" (serif, expressive) for hero + slogan moments.
- **Body / UI:** "Inter" (sans, neutral, performant) for everything else.
- **Cute accents:** "Sniglet" (rounded display) for sticker captions + badges only — used sparingly.

System fallbacks: `ui-serif, Georgia, serif` and `system-ui, -apple-system, "Segoe UI", sans-serif`.

- 🍳 sunny-side-up egg
- 🥣 cereal bowl
- 🍞 toast slice

- 🍓 strawberry

- ⭐ sparkle (5-point, soft)
- 🎮 controller silhouette
- 👜 bear-charm purse silhouette (avatar callback)
- 🧢 black cap silhouette (avatar callback)

### Icon style
- Rounded corners (4–8px)
- 2px stroke OR fully filled — never mixed in same set
- Soft drop shadow `0 2px 0 rgba(59,42,34,0.08)` for floating icons
- Pastel fills only — never primary saturated red except for live indicator

## 8. Motion Design Style
See `motion-guidelines.md` for the full system.

Headline rules:
- Default ease: `cubic-bezier(0.32, 0.72, 0, 1)` — soft, premium settle
- Default duration: 320ms (UI), 600ms (entrance), 1.6s (idle floating)
- Reduced motion: idle motion disabled, entrance animations replaced with 200ms opacity fades
- Live pulse: 1.4s ease-in-out, max 3 visible pulses concurrently

## 9. Merch Style Guide

### Foundations
- Soft-touch fabrics, oversized fits
- Print-rich fronts, clean backs (small lockup)
- Slogan + food graphic on most hero pieces
- Avatar-callback pieces (cap, sage cardigan colorway, bear-charm) reserved for premium drops

### Layout grammar
- Centered slogan stack
- Food graphic anchored low-center or right-of-text
- Wordmark or "EMG731" tab label inside hem
- Care label includes "Made for the breakfast squad"

### Color scheme rules
- Mint, pink, butter, lavender, cream are the default garment colors
- Black/charcoal allowed only as accent for caps and beanies (avatar callback)
- Avoid pure white tees — use eggshell #FFFCF5 instead

## 10. Creator/Character Representation Guide

The provided avatar is the **anchor identity**. When she appears in art, the following must hold:

- Long wavy brown hair visible below the cap
- Black baseball cap, brim forward
- Sage / light-green off-shoulder cardigan over white inner top
- Distressed light-blue jeans
- Brown/white shoes
- White purse with bear/charm accessory
- Soft glam expression — calm smile, alert eyes

### Permitted variations
- Seasonal swaps of the cardigan color (kept within pastel family)
- Holiday hat swaps (cap → beanie at winter, cap → sun visor at summer)
- Outfit drops aligned with merch collections (Waffle Stack, Egg Drop)
- Always retain hair, cap silhouette, and bear-charm purse — those are the recognition anchors

### Off-limits
- Never depict the avatar holding products that don't exist in the catalog
- Never depict the avatar in unrelated brand environments
- Never depict the avatar in sleepwear or anything that crosses into mature/sexualized styling — strict child-friendly boundary
- Never use Roblox first-party game key art as a backdrop — always original "breakfast world" environments
